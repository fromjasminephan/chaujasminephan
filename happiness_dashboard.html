Surprising findings
Political Stability ranked #1 in XGBoost
Outranked GDP per Capita (typically considered the primary driver) — suggests governance quality is the true foundation of national wellbeing.
Life Satisfaction ranked dead last (19th)
Only 1.42% gain — likely collinear with the target variable itself, rendering it uninformative for prediction despite its face validity.
Internet Access appears in both model top 5s
Digital infrastructure consistently ranked as a top happiness predictor across both RF and XGBoost — a policy lever overlooked in traditional economics frameworks.
